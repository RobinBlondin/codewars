It's 9 time!

I want to count from 1 to n. How many times will I use a '9'?

9, 19, 91.. will contribute one '9' each, 99, 199, 919.. wil contribute two '9's each...etc

Note: n will always be a positive integer.
Examples (input -> output)

8  -> 0
9  -> 1
10 -> 1
98 -> 18
100 -> 20
